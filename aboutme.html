ABOUT ME

My name is Thalyssa Ayumi, I'm from Brazil, I'm 23 years old, and I'm a level 2 student in Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College in Ottawa, ON. I use the artistic name of Ayuhara, which Ayu stands for my name, Ayumi (歩美 - the one who moves forward, who walks, in Japanese) and Hara which stands for Harajuku (原宿 - Harajuku is a place in Japan known internationally as a center of Japanese youth culture and fashion.) My passion for design began at a very early age, starting with a love for drawing. What began as scribbled drawings evolved into digital character illustrations, and eventually into logo and clothing designs. I have always had a deep admiration for art and enjoy everything related to it. This passion led me to explore various other aspects such as photography, makeup, and video production. My experience in the world of digital art has continued to evolve. When I entered college, I discovered web development and UX design, which I had unconsciously been drawn to in the previous years where I worked as IT technical support and found that customer service, user experience, and web development processes are crucial in making people's lives easier. Because of this, I am committed to being a responsive, client-driven, and artistically creative designer.
